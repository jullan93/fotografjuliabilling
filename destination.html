Destination Wedding Photographer

Halland - Sweden - Europe

My greatest joy is capturing your love exactly as it feels. Whether you're getting married at the top of a mountain, in a quiet forest clearing, or in the heart of a bustling city. I’ll be there to document the small, genuine moments. A trembling hand, a glance full of anticipation, a laugh that bubbles up. Because that's where the magic lives.

For me, it’s not just about taking photos. It’s about creating a safe and relaxed space where you can be completely yourselves. That’s when I can capture what truly matters. The memories you’ll want to return to, time and time again.

HERE’S HOW THE PROCESS WORKS:

I. First meeting & booking

It all begins when you reach out, via the contact form on my website, email or text message - choose whatever works best for you. Once I hear from you, I’ll get back with all the information you need. We’ll then schedule an initial online meeting to go through your thoughts and wishes. When you’re ready to book, I’ll send over a clear and secure agreement along with an invoice for the booking fee (50% of your selected package) so everything feels set and organized for the journey ahead.

II. Planning meeting – going through everything

Ahead of your wedding, we’ll schedule an extended planning meeting to go through all the important details – from the timeline and travel logistics to location choices, your personal wishes, and any questions you might have. This meeting is a vital part of the process, helping us build a shared vision and ensuring everything feels smooth and stress-free when the big day arrives. It’s our chance to get aligned on every detail, so you can fully relax and focus on enjoying your celebration.

III. Arrival & meet-up on location

I always arrive at your destination the day before the wedding – to be fully prepared and present, without stress. If time and light allow, we can meet in person and even do a short, relaxed shoot. This is perfect for easing into the experience and capturing a few beautiful, spontaneous moments before the festivities begin. It's also a lovely chance to connect face-to-face before the big day.

IV. The wedding day

The day has finally arrived! After all our planning and connection, it’s now your time to shine. I’ll be there for as long as you want me – quietly present but always ready to capture your most meaningful moments. Because I’ve already gotten to know you, I know what matters most – and those are the moments I’ll focus on documenting.

V. Delivery of your images

After the wedding, I carefully go through and edit all your photos. Within four to six (4-6) weeks, you’ll receive your complete gallery, filled with memories, emotion, and moments to treasure for a lifetime. If you’ve booked my largest package, Diamond, a cinematic highlight film is also included – a heartfelt summary of your day that you can relive whenever you like.
